About Us

Accreditation

COR and USCOEA

SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE PROMOTION SOCIETY (SAPS) is accredited as per Safe Food for Canadians Regulations CAN/CGSB-32.310, CAN/CSGB-32.311 (Canadian Organic Standard) and United States-Canada Organic Equivalency Arrangement (USCOEA).

EU Organic Regulation (EU)

SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE PROMOTION SOCIETY (SAPS) is recommended for recognition as a certifying authority for the EU Organic Regulation (EU) 2018/848, 2021/1698 and secondary acts by CAEQ, where it has received accreditation.

About Sustainable Agriculture Promotion Society

SAPS is the society registered under the Govt of Uttarakhand, for the organic and other certification and Organic promotion related activités.

SAPS operates under following product certification scheme(s)

  • Canadian Organic Standards (Product Regulation Compliance with the CAN/CGSB 32.310/ CAN/CGSB 32.311 General Principles and Management Standards and Permitted Substances Lists) Most Updated Version
  • EU 848/2018
  • USDA NOP (National Organic Program)

Quality Policy

SAPS is dedicated to the quality policy that will ensure that its inspection and certification services fully meet
requirements of all stake holders at all times by complying with the requirement of the standards applicable to the
operations

We provide confidence in our certification decisions through maintaining principles including but not limited to impartiality; competence; responsibility; openness; confidentiality and complaint resolution within the certification decision processes and products. We ensure that conflicts of interest are avoided, managed and objectivity of SAPS certification activities are maintained.
Continually improve services and process quality through managements, trainings and promotions of our employee.
Maintain International/national recognition of our services in support of client’s current and future requirements and expectations within stipulated time frame.
Inspection and certification processes will be subjected to continual review and improvements made to increase effectiveness of the management system defined in the quality manual.
Continually satisfy the requirements of ISO17065 and the related accreditation program and the other documents related to the various services offered by SAPS and issued by the relevant Accreditation Bodies.
This policy will be continually reviewed with respect to the changes in conformity assessment standards to ensure that it remains relevant and suitable.
