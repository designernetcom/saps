About Sustainable Agriculture Promotion Society

SAPS is the society registered under the Govt of Uttarakhand, for the organic and other certification and Organic promotion related activités.

SAPS operates under following product certification scheme(s)

  • Canadian Organic Standards (Product Regulation Compliance with the CAN/CGSB 32.310/ CAN/CGSB 32.311 General Principles and Management Standards and Permitted Substances Lists) Most Updated Version and USCOEA.
  • EU 848/2018
  • USDA NOP (National Organic Program)

Why Choose Us

Sustainability Starts Here

SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE PROMOTION SOCIETY (SAPS) is a non profit organization!

Building trust between individuals and organizations, enhancing organic certifications, and promoting a sustainable society are all part of SAPS Mission.

Our Goal is to collaborate with individuals worldwide in order to promote the credibility in implementation of organic standards and regulations in order to support the organic agriculture industry’s long-term, sustainable growth.

Fostering Trust in Organic Agriculture

Foster trust between individuals and organizations to strengthen the organic agriculture community.

Enhancing Organic Certification Credibility

Improve organic certification processes to ensure high standards and credibility.

Advocating for Sustainable Farming

Promote a sustainable society by advocating for environmentally friendly farming practices.

Global Collaboration for Organic Growth

Collaborate globally to support the long-term growth of the organic agriculture industry through strict adherence to organic standards and regulations.
